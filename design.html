our design process

Our design process

Our design process is based on our nearly 20 years experience in design, product development, and business. It focuses on every aspect of product life cycle as it is divided into 5 stages that includes 72 elements involving professionals from different fields.

1 – Understanding clients needs and goals
2 – Immersion in users world and product environment
3 – Ideation with various scenarios and product storytelling
4 – Iterations of building and testing different directions
5 – Analysis of potential product life-cycle and circular solutions
6 – Definition of products functionalities and construction
7 – Product CMF (color, material, finishing) options considering sustainable materials
8 – Cooperation with manufacturers in building high fidelity prototypes
9 – Design of product packaging and all the manuals documentation
10 – Supervision over production process

Product life-cycle mapping

Life cycle mapping is a systematic and comprehensive evaluation of the environmental, social, and economic aspects associated with the entire life cycle of a product, process, or service. Considers the various stages a product goes from raw material extraction and production, through distribution and sale to use, repairs, regeneration, and end-of-life disposal or recycling.

The primary goal of life cycle mapping is to provide a holistic perspective enabling businesses and individuals to make more informed decisions in terms of more circular product solutions.
