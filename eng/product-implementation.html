    implementation approach

    We bridge the gap between design and reality

    Great design means nothing if it cannot be manufactured. We work with an extensive network of over 350 verified suppliers and manufacturers across Europe to ensure every product we design can actually be built - at the right quality, cost and scale.

    Our implementation team guides projects from the first prototype through production ramp-up, quality control and final delivery. We speak the language of both designers and manufacturers.

    implementation services

    What we deliver

    Supplier Sourcing

    We identify and qualify the best manufacturers for your product based on material, technology, geography, capacity and sustainability criteria. Access to 350+ verified partners.

    Production Budget

    Detailed cost breakdowns, tooling estimates, MOQ negotiations and total landed cost modelling. No surprises at the production stage.

    Prototyping

    From concept models and appearance samples to functional prototypes and pre-production units. We manage the full prototyping cycle.

    Production Supervision

    On-site and remote production monitoring, quality inspection, packaging specification and logistics coordination. From first article to final shipment.

    Strategy & Pricing

    Sales strategy, retail pricing, channel planning and go-to-market support. We help position your product competitively in the market.

    Marketing Assets

    Product photography direction, sales presentations, e-commerce content and trade fair materials. Everything you need to launch confidently.

    our network

    350+ verified suppliers & manufacturers

    Built over 20 years of entrepreneurial practice, our supplier network covers a wide range of materials, processes and geographies - from precision metal workshops in Warsaw to bio-material specialists across Scandinavia.

    Every supplier is vetted for quality standards, production capacity, communication reliability and - increasingly - environmental and social responsibility credentials.
