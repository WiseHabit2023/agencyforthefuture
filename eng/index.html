our design portfolio

We design for meanings, feelings & business

Moment Watch – a minimalist, automatic timepiece

Designed by: Martyna Piaściak
Brand: Mudita
Scope of work: research, design strategy, industrial design, packaging

Modern, unisex design. Self-winding, battery-free mechanism. Carefully selected components. Interchangeable one-click straps made of vegan, plant-based materials. The additional analog breathing feature – amazingly simple yet powerful tool to help you restore your tranquility. Honored by a few international design awards.

Wise Kitchen – mobile, modular kitchen

Designed by: Karolina Ferenc, Ryszard Rychlicki
Brand: Wise Habit
Scope of work: research, strategy, industrial design, packaging, photoshoot

New approach to kitchen design. Collection of adaptive and mobile free-standing modules designed in the spirit of rethink | reduce | recycle. Easy to rearrange. Made of quality materials with repairable design. Awarded in international IF Design Award Contest 2024.

Text block – modular furniture system

Designed by: Pini Leibovich
Brand: tre product
Scope of work: industrial design, packaging, photoshoot

Modular furniture system designed by Pini Leibovich. The system permits unlimited possibilities of arranging and presenting items in a space while maintaining minimalist design and functionality.

Meditation Cushions

Designed by: Karolina Ferenc
Brand: Wise Habit
Scope of work: research, design strategy, industrial design, packaging, photoshoot

Excellent tool for meditation. Carefully designed construction provides support for the spine and an ergonomic body position. Covers made of 100% recycled acrylic, covered by a 5-year warranty.

Multicategory design refreshment

Designed by: Martyna Piaściak, Karolina Ferenc, Piotr Kasprzak, Mikołaj Markowski
Brand: NKI Group
Scope of work: design audit & strategy, industrial design

A strategic overhaul of a 50+ product portfolio spanning coffee brewing, cookware and beauty, systematised into four visual languages, delivered as 450+ CMF variants in four months.

House of Sustainable Materials – exhibition

Designed by: Mikołaj Markowski, Martyna Piaściak
Brand: Wise Habit, Library of Sustainable Materials
Scope of work: exhibition design, implementation, photoshoot

The house model and library exhibition presented at Architect@Work Warsaw and Prague, promoting the Wise Habit Sustainable Materials Library and highlighting the feasibility of ecological materials in modern architecture.

Adaptive tableware for new serving rituals

Designed by: Martyna Piaściak, Mikołaj Markowski
Brand: coming soon
Scope of work: strategy, industrial design, implementation

Design in cooperation with chefs and service managers. Modular system reducing collection size, fully supporting restaurant plating, bar shareables and in-room dining. Designed in Warsaw, produced locally in Poland.

Modular & adaptive coffee brewing collection

Designed by: Martyna Piaściak, Mikołaj Markowski, Piotr Kasprzak
Brand: Wise Habit
Scope of work: research, strategy, industrial design, implementation

A collection featuring a range of coffee-making products, designed with circular product strategies in mind, with a focus on repairability, to create products that will last for generations. Designed to create daily mindful rituals.
