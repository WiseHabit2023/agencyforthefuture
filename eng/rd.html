    our implementation portfolio

    From idea to product on the market

    1 / 6

    Vovo Glasses

    Design: Ewa Hiller
    Materials & production: borosilicate, coloured glass

    Vovo Glasses are an object of art, born from fascination with two basic geometric forms. Coloured glass elements create various hues and an illusion of changing shapes. Each cone matches each cylinder, which allows for versatility of usage and different combinations of colour and form.

    Piano Table

    Design: Ewa Hiller
    Materials & production: handcrafted solid wood, varnished or painted

    Designed around the ritual of extending the table, the tabletop extension visibly hangs within the frame. It can be unfolded easily and placed securely within the top surface. Made of solid wood, the piece has an austere structure and clean lines. Its power of expression stems from simplicity, the selected materials and the novel way of folding.

    Mood Mirror

    Design: Dorothee Mainka
    Materials & production: coloured mirror and powder coated steel frame

    The Mood Mirror collection is made up of three wall mirrors of different proportions and colours. The metal shelf embraces the mirror face and wanders up each site, while providing shelf space for small everyday objects. It embodies an immersion into a colorful life.

    Bowler Mug

    Design: Ola Mirecka
    Materials & production: moulded and fired, unglazed porcelain; full-body coloured

    Bowler Mug was designed for relaxed celebration of hot beverages. The mug has a ring that protects fingers from the hot surface and prevents it from slipping out of one's hand. It is made of specially developed porcelain that doesn't require glazing. The matt surface makes it unique and will age beautifully.

    Cork Stool

    Design: Tomasz Rygalik
    Materials & production: turned cork, expanded or natural

    Cork Stool's shape was inspired by the material it is made of. Eco-friendly, highly endurable and very light, cork still seems underexploited in furniture design. Well-balanced, geometric form corresponds perfectly with its function. Shades of brown will warm up any interior and the material used will serve generations.

    Dee Table

    Design: Fabio Hendry & Martijn Rigters
    Materials & production: anodized aluminum

    The simple form of aluminium d-shaped side table resonates its modern look. It has a very unique character that transcends onto the interior. Special advantage of anodised aluminium includes corrosion and scratch resistance.
