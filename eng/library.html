    Wise Habit Library of Sustainable Materials

    We are convinced the change starts from design and designers

    Aim of the Library

    We provide a comprehensive collection of regenerative and sustainable materials that considers various factors such as the origin of material, its lifecycle, biodegradability or recyclability potential and its application in design.

    Empowerment of designers

    We are committed to empower designers and architects with the knowledge and resources necessary to make conscious and responsible design decisions that contribute to environmental preservation and sustainable development.

    Access to Library

    Located in Warsaw, our library is both a physical & online resource for sustainable and regenerative materials.

    what you gain

    What you gain from Library
    of Sustainable Materials

    1 –

    Access to curated industry-leading innovations in regenerative and sustainable materials to inspire.

    2 –

    Discover and connect with suppliers who dare to challenge typical materials and do better things for the Planet.

    3 –

    Sample renting options of all included materials needed for your projects.

    4 –

    Consultation sessions with our experts on material-related aspects of your project.

    our library is open to the public

    Join us in working towards a better future

    Design is more than aesthetics - it's the beginning of the impact we have on the world. From the concept stage, we make material decisions that shape not only the project itself, but also its environmental footprint.

    At Wise Habit, we believe that architects, designers, and creators need a modern tool that not only supports them in making responsible choices, but also truly makes their daily work easier.

    Thanks to professional curation, expert consultations, and direct support in communication with manufacturers, the Library of Sustainable Materials gives designers the space to act. Because today, time is one of the most valuable resources.
